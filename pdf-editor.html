PDF Editor

Open a PDF and tap Edit text, then tap any text to change it. The editor detects the original font, size, style and colour and matches them. Scanned PDFs are recognised automatically. You can also add text, draw, add photos, and white-out areas.

Matching: for digital PDFs the original size, colour and font style are detected and reproduced. For scanned pages the size and colour are matched and the font is a close substitute (an image has no font data to copy exactly).
